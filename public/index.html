About the Home

This beautifully, fully renovated 4-bedroom, 2.5-bath Colonial offers more than 3,300 square feet of living space, including a spacious unfinished basement, a two-car attached garage, and a fenced backyard. Set on a corner lot in the sought-after Lee's Hill community, residents enjoy access to two clubhouses with multiple pools, tennis courts, playgrounds, and trails.

Inside, you'll find brand-new LVP flooring downstairs, plush carpet upstairs, fresh paint throughout, and a new HVAC system with dual-zone touchscreen control. The fully updated kitchen is the heart of the home: new stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, an extended island, custom cabinetry, under-cabinet lighting, a separate coffee bar, and built-in pantries. The luxurious primary suite boasts vaulted ceilings, a massive closet, and a spa-style bath with a freestanding tub, glass walk-in shower, and separate vanities.

Additional highlights include a new roof, whole-home surge protection, EV-charging readiness, a fresh-air ERV system, and exterior motion-activated security lights. Move-in ready and full of thoughtful upgrades, this home is perfect for families or professionals seeking comfort and style.
